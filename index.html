Food is Good

Eating with family (found, friends, or biological) is one of the great joys of life. Through love, enjoyment, repetition, and refinement over the years, family recipes become venerated texts. A lot of ours were kept on somewhat decaying index cards in a metal box under the stove. Inconvenient and impermanent, we decided to preserve them in a way that also offers greater ease of use! Welcome to the Harel Family Recipe Box. Food is good! Let's eat :)
