Protein Heavy Tofu Pesto

Description

Easy and healthy pesto recipe.

Ingredients

    1/2 cup raw cashews

    1 cup fresh basil (or more)

    1 cup spinach

    1 lemon's worth of juice

    1/2 package of soft tofu

    1/4 cup nutritional yeast

    Spices (cumin, garlic, onion, paprika, black pepper)

    2 cups parmesan

    Pasta

Steps

  1. Soak cashews in boiling water for 10 minutes
  2. Follow instructions to cook your noodles, being sure to reserve the pasta water
  3. Combine cashews, basil, spinach, tofu, lemon juice, spices, nutritional yeast, and about 1/4 cup of pasta water in blender. Blend until fully incorporated. Add dashes of pasta water as needed to encourage blending.
  4. Add parmesan and blend until fully incorporated.
