Ellen's Spicy Peanut Ginger Noodles

Description

These spicy peanut ginger noodles are a total crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of noodles (any kind work, but I like to use angel hair, spaghetti, vermicelli, or rice noodles!
  • 2 tablespoons sherry or apple juice
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 tablespoon hot cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter or tahini
  • 1 English cucumber
  • 1/2 cup peanuts (optional)
  • 2 scallions (optional)

Steps

  1. Follow the instructions to cook your noodles.
  2. Mix together sherry (or apple juice), rice vinegar, honey, cayenne pepper, water, soy sauce.
  3. Puree together garlic, giner, and peanut butter (or tahini)
  4. Combine the liquid mixture with the puree, and your sauce is ready!
  5. Peel and slice the cucumber into long, thin slices.
  6. Mix the sauce with the noodles and fold in cucumbers, peanuts, and scallions.
