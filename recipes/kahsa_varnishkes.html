Kasha Varnishkes

Description

This traditional Jewish recipe is true comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of Kasha (I like the medium grain, but any size will work)
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillion
  • 1 tablespoon butter (I use Earth Balance)
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 cup bowtie noodles (you can use any kind of noodle, but bowties are classic. Try alphabet noodles to make this for a kid!
  • 1 oz sliced mushrooms (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped parsely (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring water, bouillion to a boil. Reduce heat and set aside.
  2. Cook the bowtie noodles to al dente, then butter them.
  3. Follow the instructions to cook the kasha. Don't skip the egg toasting step!
  4. When you add the chicken stock (water + bouillion) to the kasha STEP BACK! It will sputter.
  5. Combine the buttered pasta with the cooked kasha.
