Classic Jewish Sugar Cookies

Description

Classic Jewish sugar cookies, just like Grandma's.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter or shortening
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 6 tablespoons milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 5 and 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 teaspoon baking powder

Steps

  1. Cream butter or shortening.
  2. Add sugar, cream well
  3. Add milk and vanilla to beaten eggs
  4. Sift dry ingredients together then add alternately with liquid ingredients to creamed mixture, starting and ending with dry
  5. Chill thoroughly
  6. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  7. Roll out batter to 1/4 inch thickness
  8. Bake about 15 minutes
