Grandma Ruth's Devil's Food Cake

Description

SarahMay's favorite recipe of all time.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 and 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa (use Dutch process)
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Sift together flour, sugar, salt, cocoa, and baking soda.
  3. Cream butter.
  4. Add sifted ingredients, vanilla, and 2/3 cup of sour cream to the creamed butter and beat together.
  5. Add the final 1/3 cup of sour cream, and the eggs, and beat together.
  6. Bake for 35+ minutes.
